Service Flow Chart
The flow of services in the company runs as follows:
 
Step 1: Once a person becomes a member of the company, he is allotted a unique membership number alongwith membership Kit.

Step 2: We collect all the key documents viz. PAN, KYC, UID, Address Proof, ID Proof etc. and get it saved on our server for the quick and easy retrieval, as and when needed.

Step 3: Includes an extensive interaction between our expert wealth managers and our member to know more about his/her psychology, product requirement, risk appetite, investment horizons etc. and working with clients to discover their personal financial objectives and assessing their entire financial situation.

Step 4: The most vital part of our Service methodology includes filling up of various working papers which allow us to know more about finances of our member in a comprehensive manner as well as our member to realize the importance of Real Financial/Wealth Planning.

Step 5: Filling up of working papers enables us to suggest our member exactly the product most suitable to his/her Requirement. Product suggestion usually includes more than one option to choose from.

Step 6: The portfolios of our members are reviewed periodically to make it compatible with market dynamics and to ascertain necessary changes in it, if any.

Step 7: Every member of the company enjoys services of a dedicated Wealth Manager for the Routing service Issues at his/her doorstep.
