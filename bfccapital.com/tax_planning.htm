Tax Planning

Tax planning refers to planning your finances in such a way to get the maximum advantages out of rules and regulations of income tax act, 1961 in the form of deductions, rebates and differential rates.

Taking advantage of Sec. 80C, 80D, 80 CCF and Sec. 54EC in the most optimal manner and saving tax in the best possible manner is included in Tax Planning.
